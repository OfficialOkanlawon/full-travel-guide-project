About the place

Paris is one of the most beautiful cities in the world. It is also known as City of Lights (La Ville Lumier). The city is also known for its high quality gastronomy and the terraces of its cafes. Paris has one of the most famous paintings in the world, Mona Lisa by Leonardo da Vinci. Paris is known for its food, fashion, art and its beautiful sites. Paris is also known as the City of Love.

What will you experience

  • Go to the Eiffel Tower and watch sunset : Here we learn about Paris culture, traditions, and history. We also have a panoromic view from the 2nd and 3rd story viewing platforms.

  • Paris Local Districts and Stories Off the Beaten Track Guided Bike Tour: Discover the rich history of the City of Light, absorb the local atmosphere.

  • Louvre Museum Skip the line Access Guided Tour: Paris Maoulin Rouge Cabaret Show with Champagne Only or Dinner: Witness the iconic Can-Can dance and much more during a 3- hour performance at the Paradis Latin in Paris.

  • Go on a big bus Paris hop-On hop-off tour: Enjoy the convenience of hop-on hop-off access at any stop along the route, and soak in the views from an open-top, double decker bus.

  • Go to Disneyland: Experience the Disney Magic you know and love with attractions, shows and parades, as well as opportunities for selfies at character meet ‘n’ greets.

  • Normandy D-Day Beaches & American Cemetery Trip from Paris: Explore Omaha Beach, the American Cemetery, and Arromanches artificial habor.

  • Versailles Monarchy Private Tour: Visit the Palace of Versailles on a private day trip from Paris. See the Hall of Mirrors, the gardens, and the Petit Trianon.

  • Catamaran from Cannes: Take in views of the fabulous French Riviera from the water on a luxury catamaran cruise.Sail along the Bay of Cannes while relaxing on deck, and splash into the sea for a swim and snorkel if you like.

  • Attend the Royal Serenade at the Chateau de Versailles
